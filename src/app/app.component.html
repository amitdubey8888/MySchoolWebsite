Curriculum & Activities

  • Objectives
    Curriculum Framework has been developed on the foundation of trust, autonomy, flexibility and accountability with the main objective to generate fresh thinking and action.
  • Curriculum Objectives

    The objective in designing in-house curriculum is to offer teaching and learning which ensures that all pupils :
    1. Are interested and motivated in their work;
    2. Achieve success and make progress in their learning;
    3. Derive satisfaction from their achievements;
    4. Have confidence in their ability to work at an appropriate level;
    5. Experience an appropriately broad and balanced curriculum;
    6. Have equality of access to learning and subjects of the curriculum;
    7. Experience a range of teaching methods;
    8. Are involved in integrated and subject -specific activities;
    9. Work individually and as a member of a group;
    10. Have their progress in the key skills of learning monitored and recorded;
    11. Work with a number of adults;
    12. Learn in a purposeful atmosphere where there is mutual respect and deep understanding;

  • Competencies Fostered

    1. Self - direction: the capacity to carry out independently and to take initiative
    2. Inventiveness: The creative use of resources, alternative methods and explanations
    3. Problem-solving involving diagnostic ability, problem formulation, problem solving skills
    4. Analytical and Evaluative Skills
    6. Decision - Making Skills: Deciding what is relevant and what is not, what to include and what to leave out
    7. Integrative Skills: The synthesis of ides, experiences, and information from different sources and disciplines.
    8. Interpersonal communication
    9. Communicating with peer group a and adults
    10. Elucidating ideas through written work, graphs, oral presentations etc.
